Storm's Tweaks Mod is a very small mod that adds some configuration options that change some small mechanics of the game.
This mod allows you to:

-Configure the kill count for the pillars

-Customise the damage variance for players and enemies (1.4.4) / Disable damage varaince (1.4.3)
-Allows for "super crits", if your crit chance is above 100% however much by is the chance to perform a super crit, which deals x3 damage as a regualr hit

-Choose how long it takes fro you to respawn (1.4.4)
-Allow you to respawn at full health (1.4.4)
-Prevent your own explosives from dealing self-damage (Doesn't work with exploding bullets for some reason)
-Prevent certain cavern traps from harming you (Doesn't work on Temple traps or any trap in "No traps" worlds (1.4.4)
-Prevent you from dropping a tombstone on death (1.4.4)
-Prevent you from being chilled in water in a snow biome in Expert mode (1.4.4)

-Allow Axes and Hammers to benefit from Mining Speed buffs.
-Allows Mechancial tools to benefit from mining speed buffs (Drills, Chainsaws, and Jackhammers)
-Allows drills to mine in a 3x3 area (1.4.4)
-Alows unsafe walls to be broken from the inside (1.4.4)
-Greatly increase your block placement speed (1.4.4)

-Allows certain boss drops to be shimmerable into eachother (1.4.4)
-Prevent your from falling through shimmer (1.4.4)

-Unlock an enemy's full bestiary entry after just 1 kill (1.4.4)
-Prevent prehardmode enemy stats from scaling in Expert hardmode 
-Disable dev items dropping from treasure bags
-Make falling stars fall as items instead of projectiles
-Make Blazing Wheels and Spike balls killable
